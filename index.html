About Me

Lisa Grupen

As a dedicated sports psychologist, I specialize in enhancing the mental resilience and performance of athletes. With over 14 years of experience, I provide tailored strategies to help individuals and teams overcome challenges, boost confidence, and achieve their peak potential. My approach integrates psychological techniques with practical coaching to foster both personal and professional growth, ensuring athletes excel both on and off the field. Passionate about mental well-being, I am committed to guiding my clients through their journey to optimal performance and holistic health.
